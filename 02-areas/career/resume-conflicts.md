# Summary

The two resumes intentionally target different roles. Use the right resume for the right application; consult this audit when you need to know what differs.

# Recommended Resolution

To maintain consistency across resumes and professional profiles:

1. Standardize employment dates across all versions.
2. Explain concurrent positions as consulting, contract, freelance, or part-time work where applicable.
3. Use consistent job titles for the same employer unless an official promotion occurred.
4. Maintain a master resume containing every role and project, then derive specialized AI and Fullstack resumes from it without altering historical facts.
5. Ensure LinkedIn, portfolio, and resume timelines align to avoid discrepancies during recruiter or background verification.
