# Master Timeline

> **Policy:** This timeline tracks employers and evidence on file. It does **not** merge the two resumes into one canonical version. Conflicts stay documented in [[resume-conflicts]] — use Resume A or Resume B depending on the application.

## Instructions

4. For application-specific dates/titles, link to the relevant resume instead of overriding here.
