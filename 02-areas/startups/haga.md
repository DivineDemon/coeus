# Haga

## Snapshot

Haga is an independent verification startup focused on robotics and physical-AI trust. The current product wedge is simulation-first benchmarking and world-model consistency checks, with public evidence published through Haga's live site.

## Stage

- Stage: early validation with shipped benchmark artifacts and active design-partner outreach.
- Positioning: trust and QA layer for robotics builders rather than a robot-model builder.

## What exists today

- Core research and benchmark engine in `[[03-resources/github-repos/haga|haga-core]]`.
- Public trust surface and metrics publishing on [haga.mushoodhanif.com](https://haga.mushoodhanif.com).
- Private web properties associated with this venture are referenced in deployments (`haga-web-site`, `haga-web-dataroom`).

## Core capabilities

- Simulation stress testing for manipulation and locomotion tasks.
- Physics-consistency checks for world-model outputs.
- Reproducible benchmark reporting and public metrics publication workflow.
