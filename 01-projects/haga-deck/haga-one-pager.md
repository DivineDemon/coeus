# Haga — One-Pager for Angel/Micro-Investors

## Thesis
Simulation is no longer the bottleneck. Trust is.
World models and robotics simulators are shipping fast; the thing missing is an independent layer that verifies physics consistency and exposes failure modes before deployment.

## What we are
Haga is the verification layer for physical AI.

We evaluate two artifacts that matter:
- Generated world / world-model video: is the physics consistent?
- Robot policy inside that world: does it survive physical stress?

Output: reproducible numeric reports with defined thresholds and shown failures.

## Evidence now
All shipped and public at `haga.mushoodhanif.com/lab`.

- Policy stress benchmarks: Lift, Stack, PickPlaceCan, Door under tiered mass/friction stress, reproducible seeds, Wilson CIs.
- Physics-checker v0: calibrated detector with 1.000 recall on violated trajectories, 0 false positives.
- Physics-IQ cohort: real-video negative control 0%, CogVideoX held-out flag rate 100% via static_hover.
