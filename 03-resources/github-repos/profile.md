### 👋 About Me

I'm **Mushood Hanif**, sole founder of **[Haga](https://haga.mushoodhanif.com)** — the independent verification layer for simulated worlds and robot policies. World models are training tomorrow's robots; Haga stress-tests their physics and reports what fails, with reproducible numbers.

Before Haga I spent **5+ years** as a Development Team Lead shipping AI-driven SaaS end-to-end — architecture through production — across Pakistan and Dubai. That same builder's posture now goes into evaluation systems, not client roadmaps.

- 🔬 Building **Haga** — policy stress + generative physics consistency for sim-first robotics
- 🤖 Deep in **AI/LLM systems** and **workflow automation** with n8n *(Verified Creator)*
- 🏗️ Previously led a **team of 10** frontend engineers across 7 concurrent projects
- 🎓 B.Sc. Software Engineering (Hons.) with a Minor in **Data Science** — GIFT University

**Live:** [haga.mushoodhanif.com](https://haga.mushoodhanif.com) · **Lab & methodology** on the site · **Core / web** repos under this account
